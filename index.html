With over 20 years of legal expertise, Advocate Rajkumar Kachhawa has built a distinguished practice rooted in trust, thorough preparation, and unwavering dedication to client interests. Based at the Court Premises in Khajuwala Village, Bikaner, he serves individuals and families across Rajasthan.

His practice spans the full spectrum of legal services — from sensitive family and divorce matters to complex land and property disputes, civil litigation, and criminal defense. Every case receives meticulous attention, clear communication, and strategic counsel tailored to achieve the best possible outcome.
